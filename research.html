Vien Ngo – Research

My research focuses on looking for the bridge among machine learning techniques with robot learning and motion planning.

Research Interest

  • Machine Learning: Reinforcement learning, probabilistic modeling, deep learning, Bayesian optimization, planning under uncertainty

  • Robotics: Motor skill learning, movement primitives, manipulation under uncertainty, model learning, optimal control, human-robot interaction.
